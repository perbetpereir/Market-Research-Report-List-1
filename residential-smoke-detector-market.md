<p><strong>The Residential Smoke Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Residential Smoke Detector</li><li>Dual-Sensor Residential Smoke Detector</li><li>Ionization Residential Smoke Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential smoke detector market comprises three primary types: Photoelectric smoke detectors, which use light beams to detect slow-burning fires; Ionization smoke detectors, which are more responsive to fast-flaming fires due to their radioactive material; and Dual-Sensor smoke detectors, which combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies for comprehensive fire detection. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the nature of the fire, enhancing safety in residential environments by ensuring early warning and effective response to potential fire hazards.</p></p>
