<p><p>The butadiene market comprises various production methods, primarily extractive distillation and oxidative dehydrogenation. Extractive distillation involves separating butadiene from hydrocarbon mixtures using selective solvents, enhancing purity. Conversely, oxidative dehydrogenation focuses on converting butane or butenes into butadiene through a catalytic process, allowing for efficient production with fewer chemicals. Both methods cater to the growing demand for butadiene in automotive, plastics, and synthetic rubber industries, highlighting their significance in the chemical landscape.</p></p>
