<p><ul><li>Expectorants</li><li>Mucolytics</li><li>Mucokinetics</li><li>Mucoregulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mucoactive agents encompass a range of therapeutic substances that help manage respiratory conditions by affecting mucus production and clearance. Expectorants facilitate mucus expulsion, promoting productive cough. Mucolytics thin mucus, making it easier to expel, which is beneficial in obstructive airway diseases. Mucokinetics enhance ciliary function and mucus transport, aiding clearance. Mucoregulators help normalize mucus secretion, addressing overproduction or deficiencies. Together, these agents play a crucial role in improving respiratory function and alleviating symptoms associated with various respiratory disorders.</p></p>
