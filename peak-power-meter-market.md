<p><p>The Peak Power Meter market is divided into two main types: Single Channel and Dual Channel meters. Single Channel meters measure power output from one source, making them suitable for basic applications or single-phase systems. In contrast, Dual Channel meters can monitor two sources simultaneously, allowing for more complex analysis and comparison of power metrics from different devices or phases. This dual capability enhances efficiency, making Dual Channel meters ideal for advanced applications in research and industrial settings.</p></p>
