<p><p>The CFRP (Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer) market is segmented into thermosetting and thermoplastic types. Thermosetting CFRP, which cures to a rigid structure upon heating, offers high strength and thermal stability, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, thermoplastic CFRP can be reshaped when heated, providing greater flexibility and recyclability, suitable for industries that require rapid processing and repairs. Both types cater to diverse applications based on their unique properties, performance, and processing methods.</p></p>
