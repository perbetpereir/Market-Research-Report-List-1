<p><p>The radiant conditioning system market encompasses two primary types: water heating and electric heating. Water heating systems utilize heated water circulated through pipes beneath the floor or through wall panels, providing efficient and even heat distribution. In contrast, electric heating systems use electric cables or mats to generate warmth, allowing for quicker installation and flexibility in room design. Both options offer enhanced comfort and energy efficiency, catering to diverse consumer preferences in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
