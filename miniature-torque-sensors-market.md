<p><p>Miniature torque sensors are crucial in various applications for measuring torque in confined spaces. Static torque sensors are designed to measure steady torque without motion, ideal for applications like assembly monitoring and quality control. In contrast, dynamic torque sensors measure torque in real-time during movement, making them essential for monitoring rotating machinery or automotive systems. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing accuracy, safety, and efficiency across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics.</p></p>
