<p><p>The Consumer Chemical Packaging market encompasses various types designed for the safe transport and storage of chemical products. Barrels are sturdy containers ideal for liquids, providing durability and security. Flexitanks are flexible, bulk liners, suitable for transporting non-hazardous liquids in standard shipping containers, optimizing space. Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) are large, reusable containers offering efficient bulk storage and transportation of chemicals, balancing capacity and accessibility. Together, these packaging solutions cater to diverse needs in the chemical industry.</p></p>
