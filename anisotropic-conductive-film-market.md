<p><strong>The Anisotropic Conductive Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chip on Glass</li><li>Chip on Flex</li><li>Chip on Board</li><li>Flex on Glass</li><li>Flex on Flex</li><li>Flex on Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anisotropic Conductive Film (ACF) market comprises various types based on application and substrate. Chip on Glass (COG) attaches chips directly to glass displays, enhancing touchscreen technology. Chip on Flex (COF) bonds chips to flexible substrates, ideal for wearable devices. Chip on Board (COB) connects chips to rigid boards for compact electronics. Flex on Glass (FOG) allows flexible circuits on glass surfaces, while Flex on Flex (FOF) enables connections between flexible circuits. Flex on Board (FOB) integrates flexible circuitry onto traditional boards for versatile applications.</p></p>
