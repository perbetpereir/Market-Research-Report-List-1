<p><p>The prosthetic heart valve and artificial heart valve market encompasses various types, including transcatheter heart valves, tissue heart valves, and mechanical heart valves. Transcatheter heart valves are minimally invasive options deployed via catheterization, suitable for high-risk patients. Tissue heart valves, made from animal or human tissue, offer a natural function and reduced thrombosis risk but may need replacement after 10-15 years. Mechanical heart valves, made from durable materials, provide longevity but require lifelong anticoagulation therapy due to thrombosis risks.</p></p>
