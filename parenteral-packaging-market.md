<p><p>Parenteral packaging encompasses various delivery systems used for sterile medications. Bags are flexible containers ideal for IV solutions. Ampoules are sealed glass containers that protect liquid contents from contamination, ensuring stability. Vials, typically made of glass or plastic, enable multi-dose access for vaccines and injectable drugs. Prefilled syringes offer convenience and reduce dosing errors. Cartridges are similar but designed for use with specific delivery devices. Ready-to-mix systems allow for on-demand preparation of medications, ensuring potency and efficacy upon administration.</p></p>
