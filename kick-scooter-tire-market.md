<p><p>The kick scooter tire market primarily features two types: solid tires and pneumatic tires. Solid tires are made from resilient materials, offering durability and puncture resistance, making them ideal for rough terrain. They require minimal maintenance but may provide a harsher ride. Pneumatic tires, on the other hand, are air-filled, offering superior shock absorption and a smoother ride on various surfaces. However, they are more susceptible to punctures and require regular air pressure checks, balancing comfort and maintenance needs.</p></p>
