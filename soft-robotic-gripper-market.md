<p><p>The soft robotic gripper market is predominantly categorized into two types: pneumatic and electric grippers. Pneumatic grippers utilize air pressure to create flexible gripping actions, allowing for gentle handling of delicate objects. They are often favored for their adaptability and simplicity. In contrast, electric grippers rely on motors and actuators to provide precise control and consistent performance. They are typically more energy-efficient and suited for automated processes, where speed and accuracy are paramount, making both types essential for various industrial applications.</p></p>
