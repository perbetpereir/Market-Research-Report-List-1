<p><p>Beacon lights are essential for signaling and safety purposes, available in three main types: LED, halogen, and xenon lights. LED lights offer energy efficiency, long lifespan, and low heat emission, making them ideal for continuous use. Halogen lights provide brighter illumination and instant warm-up but have shorter lifespans and higher energy consumption. Xenon lights deliver high-intensity light with a cooler color temperature, enhancing visibility. Each type serves specific applications, catering to varying requirements in different environments.</p></p>
