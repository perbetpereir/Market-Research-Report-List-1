<p><strong>The Aluminium Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum castings market primarily comprises three types: die casting, permanent mold casting, and sand casting. Die casting utilizes high-pressure injection to produce precise and complex shapes, often used in automotive and consumer goods. Permanent mold casting involves pouring molten aluminum into reusable molds, offering better surface finish and dimensional accuracy, suitable for medium-to-high production runs. Sand casting, the most traditional method, uses sand molds and is ideal for custom and large parts, providing flexibility in design but less precision.</p></p>
