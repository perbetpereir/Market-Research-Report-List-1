<p><strong>The Automotive Fuel Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Voltage Pull-up Type</li><li>Low Voltage Pull-down Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel pressure sensor market features two primary types: Low Voltage Pull-up and Low Voltage Pull-down sensors. Low Voltage Pull-up sensors operate by connecting the sensor signal to a voltage source, maintaining a high voltage level when the fuel pressure is below a predetermined threshold. In contrast, Low Voltage Pull-down sensors connect the signal to ground, producing a low voltage signal when fuel pressure is adequate. These types cater to different electronic control unit (ECU) requirements for monitoring fuel pressure in vehicles.</p></p>
