<p><strong>The Bluetooth Thermal Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal Thermal Printer</li><li>Thermal Transfer Thermal Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth thermal printer market is primarily categorized into two types: direct thermal printers and thermal transfer printers. Direct thermal printers use heat-sensitive paper, which darkens upon heating, making them ideal for short-term label printing without ink or ribbons. In contrast, thermal transfer printers utilize a heat transfer ribbon to produce more durable and long-lasting prints on various media types, suitable for barcodes and labels needing resistance to environmental factors. Both types are widely used for mobile printing solutions in various industries.</p></p>
