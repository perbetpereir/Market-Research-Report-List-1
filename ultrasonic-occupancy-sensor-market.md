<p><ul><li>Wired</li><li>Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic occupancy sensor market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired sensors are connected directly to a building’s electrical system, offering stable connectivity and reliable operation, ideal for large or permanent installations. Conversely, wireless sensors utilize radio frequency technology to communicate, allowing for flexible placement and easier installation without extensive wiring. This makes them suitable for temporary setups or retrofitting existing spaces. Both types enhance energy efficiency and security by detecting occupancy through ultrasonic sound waves.</p></p>
