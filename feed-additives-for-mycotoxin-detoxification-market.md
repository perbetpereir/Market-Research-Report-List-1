<p><strong>The Feed Additives for Mycotoxin Detoxification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adsorbing Agent</li><li>Bio-Transforming Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Feed Additives for Mycotoxin Detoxification market comprises two main types: adsorbing agents and bio-transforming agents. Adsorbing agents function by binding mycotoxins in the gastrointestinal tract, preventing their absorption and reducing toxicity. Commonly used adsorbents include clays, activated charcoal, and other natural substances. In contrast, bio-transforming agents enhance microbial activity to degrade mycotoxins into less toxic or non-toxic forms within the gut, promoting animal health. Together, these additives aim to improve livestock productivity and safeguard against mycotoxin-related health issues.</p></p>
