<p><p>Scanning lenses play a crucial role in imaging systems, specifically in applications requiring precise measurement and enhanced image quality. The telecentric type features an optical design that maintains constant magnification regardless of the object's distance, minimizing optical distortions. This makes it ideal for metrology and industrial inspection. Other market types may include standard or refractive lenses, which offer versatility but may compromise on precision. These alternative lenses are often used in general imaging applications where telecentricity is not essential.</p></p>
