<p><p>The metal cleaners market is primarily segmented into alkaline and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners utilize basic solutions to effectively remove organic contaminants such as oils, greases, and dirt from metal surfaces, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, acid cleaners contain acidic compounds that target rust, oxidation, and mineral deposits, often used for surface prep and etching. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining metal integrity and enhancing surface quality in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
