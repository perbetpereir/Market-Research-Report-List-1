<p><p>The tandem carding machine market is primarily divided into two types: stationary flat cards and revolving flat cards. Stationary flat cards provide a consistent and stable carding process, ideal for high-quality fiber processing. In contrast, revolving flat cards utilize a rotating mechanism to enhance production efficiency and improve fiber separation. Both types play crucial roles in the textile manufacturing industry, catering to different operational needs and preferences for fiber handling and processing efficiency.</p></p>
