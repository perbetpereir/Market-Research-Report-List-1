<p><p>The pH sensors market is categorized into several types, including glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors utilize a glass membrane to measure hydrogen ion concentration, offering high accuracy in laboratory settings. ISFET (Ion-Sensitive Field Effect Transistor) sensors employ a semiconductor to detect ion levels, known for their robustness and rapid response, making them suitable for various applications. Other types may include combination sensors, optical sensors, and solid-state devices, each serving specific industry needs and environments.</p></p>
