<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Dental Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Type</li><li>Piezoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic dental equipment market includes two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric systems. Magnetostrictive devices use a magnetic field to convert electrical energy into mechanical vibrations, allowing for effective scaling and plaque removal. In contrast, piezoelectric systems rely on crystal oscillation to produce ultrasonic vibrations, offering precise and controlled movements for enhanced patient comfort. Both types are essential in modern dentistry for efficient, minimally invasive treatments, improving oral hygiene and patient outcomes during procedures like scaling and root planing.</p></p>
