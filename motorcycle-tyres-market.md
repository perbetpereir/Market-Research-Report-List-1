<p><ul><li>Tubeless Tyres</li><li>Solid Tyres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorcycle tyre market primarily consists of tubeless and solid tyres. Tubeless tyres are designed to eliminate the inner tube, allowing for lower weight and reduced chances of blowouts, enhancing performance and safety. They can self-seal minor punctures, offering convenience for riders. Conversely, solid tyres are made from durable materials and lack air, providing a maintenance-free option with no risk of deflation. This type is ideal for specific applications where puncture risks are high, like urban settings or off-road use.</p></p>
