<p><p>Air pollution control systems encompass various technologies designed to reduce harmful emissions from industrial processes. Scrubbers utilize liquid to remove pollutants from exhaust gases, effectively capturing particulate matter and gases like sulfur dioxide. Catalytic converters facilitate chemical reactions to reduce harmful gases, primarily in automotive applications, converting them into less harmful substances. Electrostatic precipitators use electric fields to charge and collect dust and smoke particles from exhaust streams, ensuring cleaner air is released into the atmosphere. Together, they play vital roles in enhancing air quality.</p></p>
