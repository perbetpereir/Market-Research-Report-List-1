<p><p>The dental X-ray equipment market comprises two main types: digital X-ray systems and analog X-ray systems. Digital X-ray systems utilize electronic sensors to capture images, offering enhanced image quality, quicker processing times, and reduced radiation exposure. In contrast, analog X-ray systems rely on traditional film to produce images, which may require longer processing times and higher radiation doses. The shift towards digital systems is driven by their efficiency and improved diagnostic capabilities, making them increasingly popular among dental professionals.</p></p>
