<p><p>The EEG imaging systems market is segmented into three primary types: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG involves monitoring brain electrical activity using scalp electrodes, primarily for diagnosing conditions like epilepsy. Video EEG combines traditional EEG with video monitoring to correlate brain activity with physical symptoms, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. Dynamic EEG captures brain activity over extended periods, allowing for more comprehensive analysis of neurological conditions, particularly in detecting infrequent seizures or abnormal brain activity during daily activities.</p></p>
