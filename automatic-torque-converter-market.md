<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic torque converter market includes two main types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. Single-stage torque converters offer a simpler design with one fluid coupling, ideal for light-duty applications. In contrast, multistage torque converters feature multiple stages of fluid coupling, providing enhanced efficiency and performance, particularly in heavy-duty vehicles. The choice between them depends on the application requirements, with multistage converters generally delivering better torque multiplication and smoother power transfer in demanding conditions.</p></p>
