MRI-guided ultrasound systems utilize magnetic resonance imaging for real-time visualization during procedures, enhancing precision in targeting tissues. In contrast, focused ultrasound therapy devices deliver ultrasound waves to specific areas, generating heat to treat conditions like tumors without affecting surrounding tissues. Both technologies aim to improve non-invasive treatment options in various medical applications, including oncology and neurology.</p></p>
