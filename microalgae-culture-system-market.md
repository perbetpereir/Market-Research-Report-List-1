<p><p>The microalgae culture system market is divided into two main types: closed and open systems. Closed systems utilize controlled environments, such as photobioreactors, to cultivate microalgae, allowing for precise regulation of growth conditions and minimizing contamination. In contrast, open systems involve open ponds or raceways, which are simpler and less expensive but more susceptible to environmental variables and contamination. Both systems offer unique benefits and challenges, catering to different production needs and scale requirements in the microalgae industry.</p></p>
