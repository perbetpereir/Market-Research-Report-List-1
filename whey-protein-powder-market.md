<p><strong>The Whey Protein Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Concentrate Powder</li><li>Whey Protein Isolate Powder</li><li>Whey Protein Hydrolysate Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whey protein powder market includes three main types: Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC), Whey Protein Isolate (WPI), and Whey Protein Hydrolysate (WPH). WPC is less processed, retaining more nutrients and lactose, making it cost-effective for general use. WPI undergoes further filtration, offering higher protein content and minimal fats and carbohydrates, ideal for athletes. WPH is pre-digested, promoting faster absorption and reducing allergens, commonly used in medical nutrition and sports supplements. Each type caters to specific dietary needs and preferences.</p></p>
