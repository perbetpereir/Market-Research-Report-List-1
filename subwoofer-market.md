<p><p>The subwoofer market comprises active and passive types, each catering to different consumer needs. Active subwoofers have built-in amplifiers, providing a straightforward setup and enhanced performance, making them ideal for home theaters and compact spaces. In contrast, passive subwoofers require external amplification and are often favored by audiophiles seeking customization and higher power. Other subwoofer market options include powered subwoofers, which combine the features of both active and passive systems, offering versatility for various audio setups and environments.</p></p>
