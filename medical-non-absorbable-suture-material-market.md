<p><p>The medical non-absorbable suture material market is classified into synthetic and natural materials. Synthetic materials, such as nylon, polyester, and polypropylene, offer high tensile strength, resistance to degradation, and uniformity, making them ideal for various surgeries. The choice between these materials depends on factors like tissue type, healing time, and surgical requirements.</p></p>
