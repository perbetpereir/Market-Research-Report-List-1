<p><p>The Direct Screwing into Plastic market encompasses two main types of screws: thread forming screws and thread cutting screws. Thread forming screws create internal threads in plastic materials as they are driven in, enabling a secure hold without material removal. In contrast, thread cutting screws are designed to cut and remove material, producing threads as they insert. Both types are essential for applications requiring strong, durable connections in various plastic products, offering versatility and reliability in assembly processes.</p></p>
