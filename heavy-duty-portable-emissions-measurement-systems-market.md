<p><p>The Heavy Duty Portable Emissions Measurement Systems (PEMS) market consists primarily of two types: Real-Driving Emissions (RDE) PEMS and Integrated PEMS (iPEMS). RDE PEMS are designed to measure emissions from vehicles under actual driving conditions, ensuring compliance with regulations. In contrast, iPEMS combines traditional PEMS with advanced data processing and integration capabilities, facilitating real-time monitoring and analysis. Both types are crucial for assessing vehicle performance, promoting cleaner technologies, and meeting increasingly stringent environmental regulations globally.</p></p>
