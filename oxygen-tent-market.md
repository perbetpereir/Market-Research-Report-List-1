<p><p>The oxygen tent market consists of two primary types: facial tents and whole body tents. Facial tents target localized oxygen therapy, providing concentrated oxygen to patients with respiratory issues, enhancing comfort and recovery. In contrast, whole body tents facilitate systemic oxygen delivery, enveloping the entire body and often used in critical care settings or for patients needing extensive respiratory support. Both types aim to improve oxygenation, support healing, and enhance overall patient well-being, catering to diverse medical needs and settings.</p></p>
