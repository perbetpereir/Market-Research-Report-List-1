<p><p>The Tubular Membrane Filtration System market comprises four key types: Reverse Osmosis, Ultrafiltration, Microfiltration, and Nanofiltration systems. Reverse Osmosis effectively removes dissolved solids and contaminants, making it ideal for water purification. Ultrafiltration specializes in separating larger particles and macromolecules, useful in wastewater treatment. Microfiltration targets even larger particulates and is commonly used in food and beverage applications. Nanofiltration sits between ultrafiltration and reverse osmosis, addressing divalent ions and smaller molecules, frequently utilized in water softening and color removal processes.</p></p>
