<p><p>The stainless steel market is categorized into several types based on microstructure and properties. Ferritic stainless steel offers good corrosion resistance and magnetic properties, while austenitic stainless steel provides excellent ductility and strength. Martensitic stainless steel is noted for its hardness and high strength. Precipitation-hardening (PH) stainless steel combines high strength with corrosion resistance, and duplex stainless steel features a mix of austenitic and ferritic properties for enhanced performance. Other types include specialized alloys and variations for specific applications.</p></p>
